Carrot Cake Recipe

Ingredients

    eggs
    buttermilk
    white sugar
    vanilla extract
    ground cinnamon
    salt
    all purpose flour
    baking soda
    shredded carrots
    flaked coconut
    chopped walnuts
    pinapple juice
    raisins

Steps

    Preheat oven to 175 degrees (C). Grease and flour pan.
    Mix flour,baking soda, salt and cinnamon.
    In another bowl, mix eggs, buttermilk, oil, sugar and vanilla.
    In another bowl, mix carrots, coconut, walnuts, pineapple, and raisins.
    Add carrot mixture to batter and fold in well.
    Pour into pan, and bake for 1 hour. Check with toothpick.
    Allow to cool for at least 20 minutes before serving.
